Sketch Hero

A little guide to creating big things with Sketch

Sketch Hero is an eBook to help you get the most out of Sketch. From creating circles and rectangles during your first steps to integrating symbols and plugins into a powerful workflow, everything you need to know will be at your fingertips — backed up by interviews, sample files, and resources so that you can get your hands dirty and start designing right away!

Explore every feature.

When Sketch 3 is released in a few weeks it will come packed with dozens of features including symbols, art boards, grouping, masking and transforms. Sketch Hero goes into each one of them, explaining how they work and providing useful examples.

Resource files + eBook

We'll go through each feature with a clear and detailed explanation and back it up with use-cases and interviews. And because nothing beats practice, you'll get .sketch files to explore each feature and how it could fit into your workflow.
